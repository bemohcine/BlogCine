How do I set up and install Ghost Kodi Addon?

Install The Crew Repository

  1. You might need to ensure you enable sources from unknown sources within your Kodi settings.

  2. Enter the settings of your Kodi settings and then open the file manager.

  3. Choose “Add source” and click on “None.”

  4. Type in the path “https://team-crew.github.io/” and select “Done”

  5. Click on the box to highlight it and then type in an appropriate name, such as “the_crew” for this media source

  6. Click “OK.”

  7. Return to your main menu and select “Addons” on the left.

  8. Start the browser for addons by clicking on the small box icon at the top left

  9. Choose “Install from the ZIP file.”

  10. Refuse to ignore the warning message by saying “Yes.”

  11. Open the source “the_crew” and select the file “repository.thecrew-x.x.x.zip”

  12. Then, wait until a message appears that the repository was installed successfully.

If you’re unsure how to install the Kodi addon look up our tutorial, including images.

Install Ghost Kodi Addon

  1. In the addon browser, select “Install from repository.”

  2. Choose the option to install “The Crew Repo.”

  3. Select your account in the section “Video add-ons.”

  4. Choose Ghost (not Ghost TV) and click “Install” on the bottom right

  5. Check the proper installation for dependencies by clicking “OK.”

  6. Then, wait until a message confirms the Kodi addon was installed successfully.

  7. Go back into the main menu, and you will see that the Kodi extension will become available from there.

How to Use Ghost Kodi Addon

I’m not going to leave you to install Ghost! Here’s a quick overview of the procedure to make use of the addon:

To begin, click on the Addons tab on the Kodi home screen.

Go to the Video addons tab and start Ghost.

When you visit the Ghost home screen, you’ll find the following seven sections, as depicted in the picture below.

Let’s begin by exploring the first part of the Ghost Kodi addon, Movies.

There are thirteen sub-sections within Movies, such as 1Click, Popular Movies, 3-D Movies, Genre, etc. If you’d like to browse movies by genre, go to Genre. Genre section. Movies will be divided into horror, action comedy, and many more.

This addon has an additional section for TV shows, also known as television Shows. Ghost Kodi addon.

Like the Movies category, the On The Air includes sub-folders such as 1Click, On The Air, Genre, Years, Networks, and more. Open the On The Air tab if you’d like to view the schedule of popular shows that are currently airing.

Why would you want the cable box when you can get IPTV using the Ghost Kodi addon? The first step is to go to the Live TV section on the addon’s home page.

There are 17 IPTV providers, including Daddy Live, Fluxus TV, Music TV, and many more. In addition, there’s an entire new universe of channels and networks within each provider.

Trakt TV lets you track the latest, find, and share TV shows and films free! And you know what? It’s officially compatible with the Ghost Kodi addon. Open Trakt on Ghost’s homepage.

There are 17 sub-folders within it, including Movie Lists, TV Lists, TV Calendar series Watchlists, Progress, and so on. If you’re unsure about the information you’re seeking,, look at the various sub-folders of Trakt.

Are you in search of something particular? For example, a movie or show? Use the Search feature on Ghost to search for anything that comes to your mind fast.

Let Ghost match your search query with its library to create the results list. After a few minutes, you’ll get the results of your search.

Finally, there are Settings.

The settings menu in Ghost allows you to tweak the various available options, including General filters, Trakt Debrid, and others. Be careful when altering every Ghost setting if you’re unfamiliar with Kodi third-party addons.
